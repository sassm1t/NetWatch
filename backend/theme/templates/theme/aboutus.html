About us

We are students of Bennett University who have recently completed NetWatch, a packet sniffing project which helped us better grasp network protocols and the data that is sent between devices. Through this project, we were able to improve our network analysis abilities and get a greater understanding of the significance of network security.
This project is a comprehensive solution combining packet sniffing and network mapping to identify potential vulnerabilities and threats in large-scale computer networks, leveraging open-source tools and libraries to create a user-friendly and accessible web-based platform for network administrators.
